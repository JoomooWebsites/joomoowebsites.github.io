Why "JooMoo?"

At one point I found a job working for a company that wanted to pay everyone by 1099 instead of W-2. To facilitate this, they had an accountant who created an LLC for each employee that didn't have one already.

The job entailed working with Joomla and Mootools, and I wanted them to think they'd picked the right guy. So after due consideration over the period of a few days, I decided to go with "JooMoo," because it has the side benefit of being fun to say.

Since that time I have continued to work with Joomla and PHP, and have also gained some exposure to open source tools like WordPress, Drupal, jQuery, jQueryMobile, and languages such as Python, JavaScript, and have even gotten some paid experience working with Java.

And the "WebSites" Part?

They seem to be popular, but my skills and interests are not limited to the internet. ☺

Silliness aside, although I can create basic, functional websites using a variety of tools, I like sites that are minimal and simple, and moreover am more comfortable doing backend back than working on the UI.
