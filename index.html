Why "JooMoo?"

At one point I found a job working for a company that wanted to pay everyone by 1099 instead of W-2. To facilitate this, hey had an accountant who created an LLC for each employee that didn't have one already. The job entailed working with Joomla and Mootools and I wanted them to think they'd picked the right guy, so I went with "JooMoo."

Since that time I have continued to work with Joomla and PHP, and have also gained some exposure to WordPress, Drupal, jQuery, jQueryMobile, and languages such as Python, JavaScript, and have even gotten some paid experience working with Java.

And the "WebSites" Part?

They seem to be popular, but my skills and interests are not limited to the internet. ☺  Actually although I can create basic, functional websites using a variety of tools, I like sites that are minimal and simple, and moreover am more comfortable doing backend back than working on the UI.
